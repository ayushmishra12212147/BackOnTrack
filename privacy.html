Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BackOntrack (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use this website.

Information we collect

  • Contact form submissions: if you send a message, we receive the information you provide (name, email, message).
  • Usage/technical data: standard server logs and basic device/browser data may be processed by hosting providers or analytics/ads tools you enable.
  • Local storage: we store your theme preference (dark/light) in localStorage under backontrack-theme.

Cookies and advertising

If you enable Google Ads/AdSense or analytics, those services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure performance and personalize ads where permitted. Please configure consent and cookie requirements according to your jurisdiction.

How we use information

  • To provide and improve the website experience
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests
  • To measure site performance and advertising effectiveness (if enabled)

Sharing

We may share information with service providers used to host the site and process form submissions. We do not sell personal information.

Data retention

We keep contact messages as long as needed to respond and for reasonable record-keeping. Theme preference remains in your browser until you clear site data.

Questions about privacy? Contact us via Contact.
